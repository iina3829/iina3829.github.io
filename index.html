AD INFINITUM

Immersive Mirror Installation

Ad Infinitum is an immersive mirror installation that captivates you with its endless reflections of lights and visual arts. It is a walk-in mirror cube that dissolves its own boundaries. From the outside, it reflects its surroundings and blends into the environment. Once inside, mirrors on every surface - walls, ceiling, and floor - create a fully enclosed infinity space.

Light, video, and sound come together to form a continuously shifting experience. Each visitor enters alone or in small groups, closing the door behind them and stepping into a self-contained world of endless reflections.

The cube is mobile, adaptable, and designed to host a wide range of visual and sonic artworks creating everything from subtle atmospheres to high-impact immersive moments.

THE IMMERSIVE SPACE

Ad Infinitum is designed to be discovered. It attracts attention through reflection and curiosity, then rewards entry with a fully immersive interior experience.

The mirrored exterior acts as camouflage, while the interior reveals a boundless space shaped by light, motion, and sound. Each installation invites visitors to pause, explore, and experience a moment of visual infinity that becomes a focal point for shared attention, documentation, and social interaction.

COLLABORATION POSSIBILITIES

Ad Infinitum is open to artistic collaboration across disciplines. The physical structure of the cube remains constant, while the content inside can be fully reimagined.

Artists, designers, and studios are invited to create custom video works, generative visuals, interactive systems, or soundscapes tailored to the infinity environment. Content may be looping, durational, reactive, or event-specific.

Collaborations can range from single-artist installations to larger curatorial or production partnerships. The cube functions as a platform: a spatial canvas for immersive experimentation.

TECHNICAL SPECS

Visual system:
Four interior 85” displays deliver synchronized moving-image content. Video can be abstract, figurative, generative, or interactive, depending on the project.

Lighting:
Integrated LED strips enhance the mirror effect and can be programmed to complement or respond to the visual and sonic content.

Sound:
A dedicated soundscape can be added, using an internal speaker setup to create an enveloping auditory layer that supports the visual experience.

Structure:
Fully mirrored interior surfaces, including floor and ceiling. Enclosed door allows for a complete 360° infinity effect.

Footprint:
Requires a dedicated floor area and access for transport and installation. Detailed specs available on request.

EVENTS & RENTALS

Ad Infinitum is available for short-term and long-term rentals. The cube is mounted on wheels and can be transported to a wide range of venues.

It is suitable for events, exhibitions, brand activations, festivals, private functions, and public installations. Content can be adapted for each occasion, from calm ambient works to bold, high-energy visuals.
