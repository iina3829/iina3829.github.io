AD INFINITUM

Immersive Mirror Installation

Ad Infinitum is an immersive mirror installation that captivates you with its endless reflections of lights and visual arts. It's a walk‑in cube whose all interior surfaces are all mirrors. The result is an infinity mirror effect: the space appears boundless through endless reflections. Ad Infinitum is available for events and we are constantly searching for collaboration opportunities.

MADE TO SPARK CONVERSATION

each piece is a gentle disruption—something that shifts attention, draws people together, and stays memorable.

COLLABORATION POSSIBILITIES

The video work can be looping, durational, or reactive - generative or algorithmic approaches are welcome.

Collaboration with the broader production team is flexible and encouraged.
